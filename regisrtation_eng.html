Tender participant registration

Tender participant registration in procurement information systems is a prerequisite for participation in the procurement process. The process of registering a participant in an information system does not require the possession of professional knowledge and may well be carried out by the entrepreneur independently. The peculiarity lies in the fact that there are various tender systems and they are diverse in their purpose and functionality.

We will help you understand the details and features of a particular tender system you need. We will also provide support in registering and working with the system you need. Upon a specific request, we can prepare a written consultation.
